## üìã Overview

Hero Rabbit is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This privacy policy describes how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension for RabbitMQ management.

## üéØ Information We Collect

### Data You Provide

**RabbitMQ Credentials:**

- Server hostnames and URLs
- Usernames and passwords
- Connection settings and preferences

**Extension Configuration:**

- Feature preferences and settings
- Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
- Custom configurations and options

### Data We Don't Collect

<Callout type="warning" emoji="üö´">
  Hero Rabbit does NOT collect, transmit, or store any of the following:
</Callout>

- **Message Content**: We never access or store the actual content of messages in your RabbitMQ queues
- **Personal Information**: We don't collect personal data like names, emails, or contact information
- **Usage Analytics**: We don't track how you use the extension or collect usage statistics
- **Network Data**: We don't monitor your network traffic or collect network information
- **Third-party Data**: We don't collect data from other websites or services

## üîê How We Store Your Data

### Local Storage Only

**Chrome Extension Storage:**

- All your data is stored locally in your Chrome browser
- Data is encrypted and protected by Chrome's security features
- We never transmit your data to external servers
- Your data remains under your complete control

**Data Location:**

- Credentials and settings stored in Chrome's extension storage
- No data leaves your local machine
- No cloud storage or external databases used
- Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the extension

### Security Measures

**Data Protection:**

- Chrome's built-in encryption for stored data
- Secure credential handling
- No plain text storage of sensitive information
- Automatic cleanup when extension is removed

## üåê How We Use Your Data

### Extension Functionality

**Primary Uses:**

- Connect to your RabbitMQ servers
- Store your preferences and settings
- Remember your configuration choices
- Provide personalized user experience

**No Secondary Uses:**

- We don't sell your data
- We don't share your information with third parties
- We don't use your data for marketing purposes
- We don't analyze your usage patterns

### Data Processing

**Local Processing Only:**

- All data processing happens on your device
- No data is sent to external servers for processing
- No machine learning or AI analysis of your data
- No automated decision making based on your data
